My Favourite movie is Tare Zamin Par

Taare Zameen Par (translated as Like Stars on Earth) is a 2007 Indian Hindi-language drama film directed by Aamir Khan. The film centers on Ishaan Awasthi, an eight-year-old boy who struggles with dyslexia, leading to poor academic performance and misunderstandings from his parents and teachers. Ishaan's world is filled with imagination and creativity, particularly in art, but his learning difficulties are mistaken for laziness and disobedience. The narrative takes a turn when Ishaan is sent to a boarding school, where he meets Ram Shankar Nikumbh, an empathetic art teacher portrayed by Aamir Khan. Recognizing Ishaan's condition, Nikumbh helps him discover his true potential and rebuild his self-esteem through creative expression.Taare Zameen Par received widespread acclaim for its sensitive portrayal of dyslexia and the educational challenges faced by children with learning disabilities. The film won several awards, including Best Director for Aamir Khan at the 14th Screen Awards and the 2008 Zee Cine Awards. Its poignant storytelling and emphasis on empathy have made it a significant contribution to Indian cinema, highlighting the importance of understanding and nurturing every child's unique abilities.

4. Vegetable Sandwich Recipe

How to Make a Vegetable Sandwich

  1. Take two slices of bread and spread butter on one side of each slice.
  2. Place a layer of cucumber slices on one slice of the bread.
  3. Add slices of tomato and onion over the cucumbers.
  4. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and chat masala as per taste.
  5. Place the other slice of bread on top, buttered side down.
  6. Cut the sandwich diagonally and serve fresh with ketchup or green chutney.
